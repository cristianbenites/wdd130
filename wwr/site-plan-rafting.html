Rafting Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

To build revenue for my existing busines by sharing information of rafting plan and offering the company's service. To offer the best rafting service on the region.

Audience

Family vacations, events for companies teams, college celebrations, and of course people that love adventures. These customers are mainly adults aged between 18-40, but some young people and kids may also participate. They want a safe experience with much adrenaline and funny moments. They will search for beautiful places to go for a fair price. Considering most of the people have mobile devices I need the site to be fully responsive to small screens but it also needs to be awesome to use by big screen sizes such as desktop and laptops.
