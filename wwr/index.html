This is a river that is very popular with travelers. Get ready for some great thrills in the most beautiful and choppy waters. The rivers in the Squirrel Carcass Park are known for their lushness. This funny name refers to the abundance of squirrels in the area, as well as their predators such as birds of prey. This is a beautiful place where you can spend time with your family and breathe in the fresh air. But remember: this is whitewater rafting, you will still face high currents and a lot of adrenaline!
